About Us

About Us

Dr. N.R. Thiagarajan established a multi-specialty hospital in Theni, aiming to provide advanced medical care for the community. The hospital offers a wide range of modern treatments, including cardiac care, dialysis, and sophisticated surgical procedures. Additionally, it provides free healthcare services and training programs, which have had a significant impact on public health in the region. Due to Dr. N.R. Thiagarajan's dedication and efforts, the hospital has gained recognition as one of the leading healthcare institutions in Tamil Nadu.
